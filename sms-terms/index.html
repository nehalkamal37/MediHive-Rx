1. Program Description

MediHiveRx LLC provides administrative support services to pharmacies and IOD clinics. Our SMS Notifications program delivers healthcare administrative messages to patients who have provided explicit prior consent. Messages sent through this program may include:

  • Prior authorization (PA) status updates and appeal notifications
  • Copay, cost-sharing, and insurance benefits information
  • Requests for missing information related to claims or prior authorizations
  • Authentication and identity verification codes
  • General service and account notifications

2. How to Enroll

Enrollment requires your explicit prior consent through one of the following methods:

  • In-office: A staff member requests your consent and enters your mobile number into the system. You will receive an opt-in confirmation SMS.
  • Phone call: During a prescription or prior authorization discussion, a staff member requests your verbal consent. You will receive an opt-in confirmation SMS.
  • SMS self-opt-in: Text START, YES, or SUBSCRIBE to your pharmacy's MediHiveRx number. You will receive a confirmation request and must reply YES to complete enrollment.

Consent to receive SMS messages is never a condition of receiving healthcare services or using the MediHiveRx platform. Opt-in forms present checkboxes unchecked by default.

3. Healthcare Information and HIPAA

IMPORTANT: Messages sent through this program may include healthcare administrative information that constitutes Protected Health Information (PHI) under HIPAA, such as prior authorization status and insurance benefits details. Standard SMS is NOT an encrypted channel.

Such messages are sent only for Treatment, Payment, and Healthcare Operations (TPO) purposes as permitted under HIPAA (45 C.F.R. §§ 164.506, 164.522). By enrolling, you acknowledge that you have been informed that SMS is not encrypted, that you understand the associated privacy risk, and that you expressly request to receive this healthcare administrative information via SMS. Message content is limited to the administrative categories listed above; clinical notes, diagnoses, and prescription drug names will not be included in SMS messages.

4. Opt-In Confirmation Message

Upon enrollment, you will receive the following confirmation:

"MediHiveRx: You're confirmed for SMS updates about your prescriptions and prior authorizations. Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. Privacy policy: medihiverx.com/privacy-policy"

5. Opt-Out / Revoking Consent

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message. Upon receipt of your request, you will receive the following final confirmation:

"MediHiveRx: You have been unsubscribed and will receive no further SMS messages. Contact your pharmacy directly or visit medihiverx.com for support."

No further messages will be sent after a STOP request, except as required by law. If you later wish to re-enroll, you must provide fresh affirmative consent.

6. HELP Response

If you reply HELP to any message, you will receive:

"MediHiveRx: For support, contact us at support@medihiverx.com or visit medihiverx.com. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg & data rates may apply."

7. Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply to all SMS messages sent and received. These charges are assessed by your mobile carrier and are not controlled by MediHiveRx. Contact your mobile carrier for details on your messaging plan.

9. Privacy and Data Protection

Your phone number and SMS consent data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or any other purpose, except with the pharmacy or healthcare provider on whose behalf MediHiveRx is acting, with service providers who assist in delivering the SMS program under confidentiality obligations, or as required by law. For full details, review our Privacy Policy at medihiverx.com/privacy-policy.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the text messaging services.

MediHiveRx retains SMS consent records for a minimum of five (5) years as required by the TCPA statute of limitations and CTIA record-keeping guidelines.
